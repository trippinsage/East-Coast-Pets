Live Animals & Housing

We're more than a supply store — we're hobbyists who care. Our team keeps reptiles, fish, and invertebrates at home, and we bring that same passion and knowledge into the store every day. Whether you're setting up your first tank or expanding a bioactive vivarium, we'll make sure you leave with the right animal, the right setup, and the confidence to enjoy the hobby.

🐾 Pet availability varies — please call the store before your visit or message us on Facebook for the latest updates on what's in stock.

From beginner-friendly community species to specialty imports, we stock a rotating selection of healthy freshwater and saltwater fish. We also accept locally bred fish and work with regional breeders to bring you unique, hardy stock you won't find in chain stores.

  • Tropical community fish (tetras, guppies, mollies, corydoras)
  • Cichlids, angelfish & gouramis
  • Bettas & fancy goldfish
  • Saltwater species (clownfish, tangs, damsels & more)
  • Locally bred & specialty imports
  • Special orders available — just ask

Our reptile selection focuses on species we know well and can support with proper husbandry advice. Every animal comes with care guidance from staff who keep these species themselves.

  • Ball pythons & corn snakes
  • Crested geckos & leopard geckos
  • Bearded dragons
  • Blue-tongued skinks
  • Seasonal & special order species available

Amphibians make fascinating, low-maintenance pets and we carry a curated selection along with everything needed to build a proper habitat.

  • Axolotls
  • Tree frogs (White's, red-eyed)
  • Painted frogs
  • Fire-bellied toads
  • Pacman frogs

From cleanup crews to display animals, our invertebrate selection covers both aquatic and terrestrial species.

  • Freshwater shrimp (cherry, amano, ghost)
  • Snails (nerite, mystery, ramshorn)
  • Hermit crabs
  • Tarantulas & scorpions (seasonal)
  • Isopods & springtails for bioactive setups

We carry a selection of companion birds and everything you need for their care — from seed and pellets to cages and toys.

  • Budgies & cockatiels
  • Finches & canaries
  • Conures & lovebirds (seasonal)
  • Full line of cages, perches, food & enrichment

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what we hear most. Still have questions? Call the store — we’re happy to help.

Where is East Coast Pets located?

We’re at 256 Memorial Drive, Clarenville, NL A5A 1N9 — right alongside Canada Post, at the far end of the Co-Op parking lot. Just 2.4 km off the Trans-Canada Highway (Route 1) via Manitoba Drive.

What are your store hours?

Mon – Wed & Fri: 12:00 PM – 7:00 PM  •  Thursday: 12:00 PM – 8:00 PM
Saturday: 12:00 PM – 6:00 PM  •  Sunday: Closed

What live animals do you carry?

Our stock rotates regularly. We typically carry freshwater and saltwater fish, reptiles (ball pythons, leopard geckos, crested geckos, bearded dragons), amphibians (axolotls, tree frogs), invertebrates (shrimp, snails, tarantulas), and birds (budgies, cockatiels). Call ahead or check our Facebook page for live availability.

Do you offer aquarium maintenance services?

Yes! We provide professional aquarium cleaning and maintenance for homes and businesses in the Clarenville area — freshwater and saltwater. View our service packages or call to book your first visit.

Can you special-order animals or supplies?

Absolutely. If we don’t carry it, we’ll do our best to source it. This includes specific animal species, specialty food brands, and hard-to-find equipment. Call us or message us on Facebook to discuss your request.

What premium pet food brands do you stock?

We carry a hand-picked lineup including Oven Baked Tradition, Boreal, Bold By Nature, Crumps Naturals, Tiki Cat, PureBites, Jay’s Gourmet, Greenies, ZuPreem, and many more. We prioritize natural, high-quality nutrition across all species. Come in to browse the full selection.
